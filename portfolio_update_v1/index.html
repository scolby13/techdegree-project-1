Hello, I’m Mario.

My home is the Mushroom Kingdom!

Background

I’m a career plumber who loves to save the day. I travel to many different worlds helping my friends out of precarious situations. In my free time, I like to race karts and have parties with all my favorite people.

I'm always open for a new adventure and look forward to saving my friend, Peach, from Bowser!

Skills

I am always trying to master the levels I come across. I am an excellent jumper, coin grabber, and brick breaker. I always have my eye out for these items:

  • Fire Flower
  • Mushroom
  • Tanooki Suit
  • 1UP

Sometimes I enlist the help of my brother, Luigi. Also, my friends Toad and Toadette help when they can.

Here are some of my favorite people:

  • This is my Brother, Luigi. He is also a plumber. He can jump higher than me, but he can also be frightened a bit more easliy than I can.

  • This is Toad. He is very loyal and strong. He cannot jump high, but has a very strong kick!

  • Here is Peach. She is the princess of Mushroom Kingdom. She can jump high and glide through the air high above the land.
